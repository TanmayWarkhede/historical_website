The Medieval Era

Explore the fascinating world of knights, castles, and chivalry.

Overview

The Medieval Era, also known as the Middle Ages, lasted from the 5th to the late 15th century. It was marked by the feudal system, the rise of chivalry, and the influence of the Church.

Key Features

  • Feudalism and social hierarchy
  • The Crusades and religious conflicts
  • Gothic architecture and cathedrals
  • Chivalric codes and knighthood
  • Emergence of towns and trade

Notable Figures

  • Charlemagne - King and Emperor
  • William the Conqueror - Duke of Normandy
  • Geoffrey Chaucer - Poet and author
  • Joan of Arc - Military leader
  • Thomas Aquinas - Philosopher and theologian
