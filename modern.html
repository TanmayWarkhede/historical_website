The Modern Era

Explore the world of technological advancements, globalization, and contemporary society.

Overview

The Modern Era is characterized by rapid technological advancements, urbanization, and a globalized economy. It began in the late 19th century and continues to the present day.

Key Features

  • Technological innovation
  • Globalization
  • Rise of digital media
  • Environmental and social challenges
