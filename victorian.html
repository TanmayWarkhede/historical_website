The Victorian Era

Explore the fascinating era of industrialization and the British Empire.

Overview

The Victorian Era, named after Queen Victoria, lasted from 1837 to 1901. It was a period of immense industrial progress, social changes, and the expansion of the British Empire.

Key Features

  • Industrial Revolution
  • Colonial Expansion
  • Social reforms and progress
  • Art, literature, and innovation

Notable Figures

  • Queen Victoria
  • Charles Dickens - Author
  • Charles Darwin - Naturalist
  • Isambard Kingdom Brunel - Engineer
  • Florence Nightingale - Nurse and social reformer
